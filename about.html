About Us

Our Mission

We aim to make a small but meaningful difference by helping connect surplus food with people and organizations that need it. Our goal is to create a simple platform that encourages food sharing in real time and at a local level.

Our Team

We're a small team of students learning and building together. Each of us brings different strengths—from coding to design—and we're trying to apply what we've learned to build something useful and socially impactful.
